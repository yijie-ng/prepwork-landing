Kihon (Basics)

Punches, kicks, blocks, and stances, for the first part of the training session.

Kumite (Sparring)

Offense and defense, for the second part of the training session.

Kata (Forms)

A series of standardized movements or forms, which students must perform correctly to pass the belt tests.
